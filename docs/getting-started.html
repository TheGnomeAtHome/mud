🎯 Getting Started

Your First Steps in the World

Welcome, Adventurer!

Welcome to your first adventure in our MUD (Multi-User Dungeon)! This guide will help you get started with the basics of gameplay.

📝 Creating Your Character

Step 1: Sign Up

When you first visit the game, you'll see a login screen. Click "Sign Up" and create an account with your email and password.

Step 2: Choose Your Name

Pick a unique character name that will represent you in the world. This will be visible to other players!

Step 3: Select a Class

Choose from several character classes, each with unique strengths:

  • Warrior - High strength and constitution (STR+3, CON+2)
  • Rogue - Quick and charismatic (DEX+4, CHA+2)
  • Wizard - Intelligent spellcaster (INT+4, WIS+1)
  • Priest - Wise healer (WIS+3, CHA+2)
  • Paladin - Balanced holy warrior (STR+2, CON+2, WIS+1, CHA+1)
  • Ranger - Skilled tracker (DEX+3, STR+1, WIS+2)

🗺️ Understanding the World

The game world is made up of interconnected rooms. Each room has:

  • A name and description - Setting the scene
  • Exits - Directions you can travel (north, south, east, west, up, down)
  • NPCs - Characters you can interact with
  • Monsters - Creatures you can fight
  • Items - Objects you can pick up

⌨️ Basic Commands

All commands are typed into the command prompt at the bottom of the screen. Here are the essential ones:

🚶 Movement

  • north, south, east, west - Move in a direction
  • up, down - Climb stairs or ladders
  • n, s, e, w - Quick shortcuts for directions

👀 Looking Around

  • look - See the current room description
  • examine [object] - Look closely at something
  • inventory or inv - See what you're carrying
  • who - See all players currently online in the game

💬 Communication

  • say [message] - Talk to everyone in the room
  • tell [player] [message] - Private message to a player
  • talk to [npc] - Speak with an NPC
  • ask [npc] about [topic] - Ask NPC about something specific

🎒 Items

  • get [item] - Pick up an item
  • drop [item] - Drop an item from inventory
  • use [item] - Use a consumable item
  • equip [weapon/armor] - Equip gear

📊 Understanding Your Stats

Your character has six core attributes that affect gameplay:

  • STR (Strength) - Physical damage in combat
  • DEX (Dexterity) - Dodge chance, critical hits, accuracy
  • CON (Constitution) - Damage reduction, survivability
  • INT (Intelligence) - Magical damage and spell power
  • WIS (Wisdom) - Dodge chance, perception
  • CHA (Charisma) - Social interactions (future features)

You also have:

  • HP (Hit Points) - Your health, when it reaches 0 you die
  • MP (Mana Points) - Energy for casting spells
  • XP (Experience) - Gain XP to level up
  • Gold - Currency for buying items

🎯 Your First Quest

Here's a recommended path for new players:

  1. Explore The Nexus - Your starting area. Look around and get familiar with the commands.
  2. Talk to NPCs - Many NPCs offer quests or sell items. Try talk to [npc name]
  3. Find a Quest - NPCs with quests will tell you about them. Use quests to see active quests.
  4. Gather Basic Gear - Look for items lying around or buy basic equipment from shops.
  5. Fight Your First Monster - Find a low-level creature and type attack [monster]
  6. Level Up! - Gain XP from combat and quests to increase your level.

💡 Quick Tips for Beginners

  • 💚 Keep your HP up - Rest or use healing items if hurt
  • 💰 Save your gold - You'll need it for better equipment
  • 🗺️ Make a map - Draw out the world on paper to avoid getting lost
  • 👥 Team up - Join a party with other players for tougher challenges
  • 📜 Read descriptions - Important clues are often hidden in room descriptions
  • 💬 Ask for help - Other players are usually friendly and helpful!
  • ⚔️ Start small - Don't tackle powerful monsters until you've gained some levels
  • 🎒 Manage inventory - Drop items you don't need to make room for better loot

🆘 Getting Help

If you need assistance in-game:

  • help - Show available commands
  • help [command] - Get details on a specific command
  • say Can someone help me? - Ask other players
  • Check these guides for detailed information!

🎮 Ready to Play?

Now that you know the basics, you're ready to begin your adventure! Remember:

  • Take your time exploring
  • Experiment with different commands
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes
  • Have fun and make friends!
