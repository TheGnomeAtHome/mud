Combat Basics

Combat in our MUD is tactical and immersive, with verbose descriptions that bring every battle to life! This guide will teach you everything you need to know to become a formidable warrior.

üìä Combat Attributes

Your character's six attributes all play a role in combat:

Attribute Combat Effect
STR (Strength) Main damage stat for physical attacks (+3 damage at STR 16)
DEX (Dexterity) Accuracy bonus, critical hit chance (5%+), dodge chance
CON (Constitution) Reduces incoming damage, improves survivability
INT (Intelligence) Magical damage (for spells)
WIS (Wisdom) Improves dodge chance
CHA (Charisma) Future features (morale, intimidation)

üó°Ô∏è Weapons

Equipping weapons dramatically increases your damage output:

Using Weapons

  • equip sword - Equip a weapon from your inventory
  • inventory - See what weapons you have
  • Weapon damage is added to your base damage
  • The game auto-uses your best weapon in inventory

Weapon Types:

  • Swords - Balanced damage, versatile
  • Axes - High damage, slower
  • Daggers - Fast, good for high-DEX characters
  • Maces - Heavy crushing damage
  • Spears - Good reach and piercing damage
  • Staves - For magic users

üõ°Ô∏è Shields & Blocking

Shields provide active defense against attacks!

Shield Mechanics

  • Block Chance: 15% base + 2% per DEX point above 10
  • Maximum Block: 40% chance
  • Damage Reduction: 50-100% of attack blocked
  • Requires: Shield equipped in inventory

Shield Types:

  • Wooden Shield - Basic protection (150g)
  • Iron Shield - Better block chance (300g)
  • Steel Shield - Excellent protection (600g)
  • Tower Shield - Maximum defense (1000g)

üí¢ Damage Severity

Combat descriptions change based on how much damage you deal:

  • Light (1-3): "grazes", "scratches", "clips"
  • Moderate (4-7): "strikes", "hits", "wounds"
  • Heavy (8-12): "smashes", "pounds", "crushes"
  • Severe (13-20): "devastates", "pummels", "mauls"
  • Devastating (21+): "annihilates", "obliterates"

üéÆ Combat Flow

Here's what happens each round of combat:

  1. You attack ‚Üí Enemy dodge check
  2. If hit ‚Üí Calculate damage with bonuses
  3. Enemy counter-attacks ‚Üí Your dodge check
  4. If not dodged ‚Üí Shield block check
  5. If not blocked ‚Üí Armor reduces damage
  6. Take damage ‚Üí HP decreases
  7. Repeat until victory or defeat!

üí° Combat Strategies

Tank Build (High CON)

  • Equip plate armor for maximum protection
  • Use a shield for extra defense
  • Take on multiple enemies at once
  • Best for: Warriors, Paladins

Damage Dealer (High STR)

  • Focus on best weapons available
  • Light armor for mobility
  • End fights quickly with high damage
  • Best for: Warriors, Rangers

Evasive Fighter (High DEX)

  • Rely on dodging rather than armor
  • Use fast weapons like daggers
  • High critical hit chance
  • Best for: Rogues, Rangers

Balanced Fighter

  • Medium armor + shield combo
  • Balanced stats for versatility
  • Good for all situations
  • Best for: Paladins

‚ö†Ô∏è Death & Respawn

When your HP reaches 0, you die!

  • You respawn at The Nexus (starting area)
  • You lose 10% of your gold
  • You lose ALL items in your inventory
  • Your HP is fully restored

Tip: Store valuable items safely before dangerous fights!
